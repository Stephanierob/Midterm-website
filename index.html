The Design Process

We choose our characters, a women and a men. We cut them out in a piece of paper then we traced their shape in cardboard so the paper wouldn't fold and stand straight. After all the characters where done we decided to move to headings and add 4 little headings to explain what was happening in that section of the animation. We then cut out a cardboard that would be the stage and would hide all the motors and just show the characters and headings. After we where done with the setup of the stage we moved on to how the characters would move to show the story, we used motors glued to the characters and heading and we made a code that would allowe them to hide under the stage and when commanded to they would come up and show the story, once done with their story they would go down again as well as the headings.

This code was to move the motors that would move the characters and headings. The code was programmed to move from left to a 90 degree angle or right to na 90 degree angle. That would help the charcter move from hiding behind the stage to showing up above it. The headings would move from one heading to another just by spinning 180 degrees, they wouldmove as the story would go on to explain that part of the story and give context.
